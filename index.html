About us

Saltayan is a brand of all the natural, pure Himalayan rock salt products made from the original rocks, mined from the Himalayan belt of mountains. We craft our products in such a way that it doesn’t disturb the natural essence and the quality is always taken care of. As a brand, we thrive to deliver utmost satisfaction to our clients and provide with valued services for our vast range of products.


We make sure our products are verified and accredited by the respective corporations and also the quality standards are met. We have been successful in specialising in the areas of rock salt and are building our roadway to keep on improving the standards of our products. Saltayan products for a healthy life!
